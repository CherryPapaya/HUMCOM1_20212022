Percy Jackson and the Olympians

by Rick Riordan


"Percy Jackson and the Olympians" is a pentalogy series of novels* following the fantastical
adventures of a young demigod boy, Percy Jackson. The book series is based on Greek
mythology. In it, Percy and his friends save the world from the viscious forces of evil and slowly
learn that even misfits like them have a place in this cruel world.

*I decided to go for a book series since I love all the books equally and can't decide which one's my favorite.

- Jeremiah Mateo
